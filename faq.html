Frequently Asked Questions

How to Schedule a Flight Booking?

+ -
  • You will need to go Wohoo Homepage and select Packages. Next, you will need to press "Book Now" to start booking your flight.

How to Create A Wohoo Account?

+ -
  • First of all, you will need to go to Wohoo Homepage and select the account icon. After that, you will be directed to a page where you are able to sign in or sign up for a Wohoo account.

Where is the Feedback Form?

+ -
  • The Feedback Form is located at the Reviews tab. When you press Reviews, you will be directed to Feedback Form.

How do you give your Feedback and Review?

+ -
  • Select the Reviews tab first. Then, enter your name, email ID, phone and message to be delivered.

Where is the Contact Us Page

+ -
  • The Contact Us Page is located at the About tab. Once you selected it, it is located at the about us section. Then, click on it.

Does Wohoo sell any products?

+ -
  • Yes. Wohoo sells some products. One of the products sold is a sunglasses. To buy the products, you should select the Shops tab.
